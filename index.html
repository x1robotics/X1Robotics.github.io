Background


My name is Prescott Rynewicz, and I was born and raised in San Jose California. I attended Leigh High School, and I am currently a third year Aerospace Engineer and Computer Scientist at UCLA. I am passionate about everything Aerospace, as well as software engineering, physical computing, control systems, and general robotics. I haved worked in fields that vary from Cubesat System engineering to Chemical Catalysis Research, with many areas in between.


Outside of engineering and research, I am a passionate musician. I am the president of UCLA's only All Male A Cappella group, Bruin Harmony, and the founder of the UCLA A Cappella Council. I am also a member of the Lambda Chi Alpha Fraternity.


This site serves as an professional profile to display my work experience and project updates.




Work Experience and Leadership
