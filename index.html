X1 History and Mission Statement


X1 Robotics was founded in the fall of 2015 by Prescott Rynewicz, an Aerospace Engineer and Computer Scientist at UCLA. X1's goal is to develop challenging Robotic systems from scratch in order to develop real world problem solving and engineering skills.


At X1, we use the term Robotics loosely, in the sense that we are not just committed to building robots, but to the idea of lifelong learning and self motivation through the design and devlopment of engineered vehicle's, whether that is a small RC car, or a Free Throw Shooting Robot, or a Semi-Autonomous Drone.


X1 is primarily an undergraduate organization, but is open to all UCLA students and anyone who is passionate about engineering and excited to be a part of team that takes ideas and turns them into reality.
